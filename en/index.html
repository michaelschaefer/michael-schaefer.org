Great that you found your way to my website! I use this little blog to publish articles on a regularly unregular basis. Topics vary strongly according to my interests. This mainpage always includes a list of the most recently published articles. That way you can see quickly if there is something new. The ones who want to stay tuned even faster and more uncomplicated can use one of the following feeds: RSS, Atom.

A note about the handling: With the three half circle shaped buttons at the very top you can switch the language (left and right) and hide resp. show the banner (middle).

Notes

  • I use disqus as commentary platform. To prevent you from the necessity of creating an account there, I exclicitely allow to comment as a guest (thus without registration).

Have fun on my website!
